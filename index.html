Data Visualization & Management

Data has become the most valuable resource in the modern digital age. Many large tech companies rely heavily on the intrinsic value of the data they possess and continuously look for new ways of analyzing and visualizing that data as well. Understanding how truthful that data is, as well as how much reliance a company can place on it, is an incredibly important undertaking.

Purely quantitative analysis is only part of the picture; companies must engage business and executive level personnel to recognize the patterns in this data to make accurate assumptions and predictions. Presenting this information or data to non-technical people or employees is extremely relevant to maximizing the positive impacts of aggregating and analyzing ever greater volumes of data. This process is exemplified during product development, maintenance, customer experiences, fraud detection and compliance, new innovations, machine learning/AI, and many more areas of business. I personally spend a considerable amount of time learning to understand these principals and work on applying them to make me a better developer and innovator.
